Our Services

Garbage Management

Efficient waste collection and disposal are key to maintaining a clean and healthy city. Our platform empowers citizens to instantly report issues like garbage overflow or unattended bins, ensuring quick municipal response. By enabling faster action and greater accountability, we help keep streets cleaner, surroundings healthier, and cities more sustainable

Streetlight Maintenance

Well-lit streets play a vital role in ensuring public safety and community security. Through CivicSense, citizens can easily report non-functional or flickering streetlights with just a few taps. These reports are instantly directed to municipal teams for quick action, ensuring timely repairs and consistent illumination across the city. Together, we can make our streets safer, brighter, and more welcoming for everyone.

Water Leakage Control

Detecting and reporting water leaks is essential to prevent wastage and ensure a reliable water supply for every household. With timely reporting, our platform helps municipal teams respond quickly, fix leaks efficiently, and conserve valuable resources. Together, we can promote sustainable water management and secure a cleaner, greener future.

Road & Pothole Repair

Report damaged or uneven roads in your area to help authorities identify and prioritize maintenance work efficiently. By ensuring timely repairs, we can make roads smoother, safer, and more reliable for everyday commuting, improving travel experiences across the city.
